Andrew Zbigniew Szydlo

Performance Chemist and Educator

Biographical note

When I used to give chemistry lessons to friends some 50 years ago, I would bring with me a pocket-full of chemicals to illustrate the principles of chemistry. Today, these chemistry lessons have evolved into demonstration lectures which I give to audiences in a wide variety of locations. During the past 40 years I have given over 500 talks, in addition to teaching chemistry full-time at Highgate School in London. Today this continues to be the base for all my talks.
