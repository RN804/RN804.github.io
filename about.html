Andrew Zbigniew Szydlo

Performance Chemist and Educator

Biographical note

When I used to give private chemistry lessons to friends some 50 years ago, I used to bring with me a pocket full of chemicals to illustrate the principles of chemistry. Today, these private chemistry lessons have evolved into live demonstration lectures which I give to widely different audiences in a wide variety of locations. During the past 50 years I have given over 500 talks, in addition to teaching chemistry full-time at Highgate school in London. Today this continues to be the base for all my talks. I shall be pleased to give a talk in any school, university, institution or any other organisation. Currently I give talks in English and Polish, but if a suitable opportunity presented itself, with about 6 months’ notice, I could deliver these in Russian, French, German, Spanish or Italian !
